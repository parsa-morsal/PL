reuters is a benchmark dataset for document classification. to be more precise, it is a multi-class (eg there are multiple classes), multi-label (eg each document can belong to many classes) dataset. it has 90 classes, 7769 training documents and 3019 testing documents. it is the modapte subest of the reuters-21578 benchmark (source). the mean number of words per document, grouped by class, is between 93 and 1263 on the training set. the training set has a vocabulary size of 35247. even if you restrict it to words which appear at least 5 times and at most 12672 times in the training set, there are still 12017 words.
